FAQ

How do I use this application?

Just Sign Up and start borrowing and lending out things.

How do I reset my password?

Just click on the "Forgot Password" button on the Login Page, enter your email and you will get the reset password email right away.

How do I borrow things?

From your Dashboard, click on Explore and you will find all the available items you can borrow. Click on any item and press the "Heart" icon. Another way is to search for an item using the Search feature in the left-side menu. You will be notified when your request gets accepted by the item's owner.

How will I get my loaned out items back?

The application will send out reminder emails to the borrower mentioning to return your item, automatically. The borrower starts receiving the emails 2 days before the return date till 2 days after the return date.

I still haven't received my loaned out item, what do I do?

If it has been more than 2 days from item's return date and you still haven't recieved the item back, kindly contact the borrower yourself. This application is based on trust system and shouldn't be held responsible for the returns of users' items.

How do I make friends in the application?

From your dashboard or left-side menu, click on "Social". The Social Centre provides you the options to see your current, mutual friends along with the friend requests sent and recieved.
